# Synthetic Form

A Synthetic form is a replacement to the standard html form that allows for nested forms, focus control & disabling.

Note: When using a Form inside of a Modal the Modals focus control & disable control will override the Forms. 
When using Forms this way rely on the Modal to control the focus and disabling or your Form.
